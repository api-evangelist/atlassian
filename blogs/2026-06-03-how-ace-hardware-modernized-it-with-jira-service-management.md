The 102-year-old hardware cooperative Ace Hardware replaced email-based ticketing with Jira Service Management, integrating it with Salesforce, Azure, and other enterprise systems across 5,200 stores. The deployment enabled automated workflows and standardized IT service processes for a complex retail network that spans thousands of independently owned locations. The case study shares lessons learned from implementing modern ITSM tooling at enterprise scale within a distributed, franchise-style business model.
