We see DVCS as a transformative shift in the way software is developed. Needless to say, Bitbucket has a lot invested in DVCS. This is why we are excited to announce that Atlassian has donated $24,000 USD to sponsor Mercurial development for 2012. Our contribution goes to the Mercurial Fellowship Project, which is managed by the Software Freedom Conservancy.
