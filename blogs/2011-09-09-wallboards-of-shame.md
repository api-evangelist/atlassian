We've been using wallboards for ages now in the JIRA team to keep an eye on our builds. This worked well to give us an overall status of what builds are green and which builds are broken: It was a complete fail however when it came to assigning blame to the people that broke the build. Builds would stay red for a long time because no-one was looking into fixing them.
