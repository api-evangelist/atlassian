DevOps engineers need a way to carefully apply their changes to production. They also need to review those changes with other stakeholders, mentor fellow colleagues, and of course remember what they did six months ago.Tools & ProcessDevOps people, sysadmins and build engineers can use Agile project management practices like Kanban through the GreenHopper Rapid Board with JIRA, leverage development source control, change review tools like Crucible, and document change through Confluence.
