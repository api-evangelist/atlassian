Atlassian has been recognized as a Strong Performer in The Forrester Wave: Strategic Portfolio Management Tools, Q2 2026, achieving 5/5 scores in Vision and Innovation categories. The recognition reflects Atlassian's strategy of connecting work and AI through a unified platform that integrates Jira, Confluence, and Rovo across portfolio planning and execution. The report highlights the importance of connected operating models for enterprise-scale strategic portfolio management.
