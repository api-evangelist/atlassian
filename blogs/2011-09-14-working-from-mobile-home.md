Last June I unexpectedly found myself living in Scotland. This pushed the commute to my place of work as a Confluence Developer in Sydney to just a fraction short of 17,000km. After a few days this became a little unmanageable and I decided to convert one of the bedrooms in my house to a home office. This was going well however with baby number two due in about three weeks time, I had to finally succumb to the gentle persuasion that my home office needs to become a nursery.
