AI agents are now integrated as assignable, mentionable teammates within Jira workflows. The article explains how to assign work, mention agents in comments, connect third-party agents, and scale them through automations, covering scenarios like overnight bug fixes, quarterly planning, and campaign launches, plus setup instructions for building custom agents using Rovo Studio.
