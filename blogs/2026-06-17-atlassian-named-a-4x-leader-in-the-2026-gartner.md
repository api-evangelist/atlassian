Atlassian achieved Leader status in the 2026 Gartner Magic Quadrant for DevSecOps Platforms with the highest execution ranking. The company emphasizes four strategic bets: embedded agents throughout the SDLC, the Teamwork Graph context engine, AI visibility and governance through DX integration, and enterprise security foundations including FedRAMP authorization for regulated workloads.
