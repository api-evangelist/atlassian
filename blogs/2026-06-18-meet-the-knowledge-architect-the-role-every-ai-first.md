This article introduces the Knowledge Architect role as essential for AI-first organizations, defining its purpose as architecting systems that let AI tools access organizational knowledge effectively and upskilling teams. Five core responsibilities include designing context flow, defining human-AI engagement rules, managing knowledge infrastructure, and establishing responsible AI frameworks with transparency and accountability.
